A.Len Architectural Bureau

A.Len is an architectural studio and general design contractor established by Sergey Oreshkin in 1991

About

For development and construction companies A.Len Architectural Bureau offers its many years of experience in designing buildings and constructs to create facilities distinguished by features of brand architecture:

  • Designs a beautiful and functional building.
  • Achieves a “happy medium” between low cost and high grade architecture of a residential building, office center or shopping complex.
  • Brings life to stone, metal and glass.
  • Fits a new building into the existing environment and context, highlighting the building among similar ones and giving it individuality and originality.
