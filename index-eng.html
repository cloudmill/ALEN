A.Len Architectural Bureau

A.Len is an architectural studio and general design contractor established by Sergey Oreshkin in 1991

About

A.Len Architectural Bureau offers its development and construction client companies many years of experience in designing buildings and constructs to create facilities having distinctive features of brand architecture:

  • Designs a beautiful and functional building.
  • Achieves a “happy medium” between low cost and high grade architecture f a residentialo building, business center or shopping complex.
  • Gives life to stone, metal and glass.
  • Fits a new building into the existing environment and context, highlighting the building among similar ones and giving it individuality and originality.
